Project Proposal Presentation Outline (Sample, Due Friday July 10)

*   Project name
    
*   The problem: what's the problem you are trying to solve
    
*   Literature review (3-4 bullets): what existing work/approaches have shown, the state of the art and its reported results, and most importantly, the gap your project addresses that prior work doesn't.
    
*   Proposed solution: what your approach does differently, in plain language, with one simple visual
    
*   Preliminary findings: your strongest early evidence. E.g., prototype accuracy on a sample dataset, a feasibility test result, or pilot user/ai feedback.
    
*   Success metrics: 1-3 evaluation metrics
    
*   Plan: timeline milestones, top 1-2 risks with mitigations
